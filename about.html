ABOUT US

Network Management, Inc. (NMI) is a technology management, consulting and outsourcing company that builds, manages, secures and supports Information Technology infrastructure. When your Information Technology infrastructure is aligned with your business goals you are able to strategically improve your organizations performance in many areas such as customer service, sales and marketing, administration and more. Our decades of Information Technology experience and top notch customer service gives us the ability to help you leverage IT in efficient results-oriented ways that allow you to be more responsive, efficient, productive and profitable.

Since our founding in 1994 we have helped hundreds of companies and organizations nationwide build, manage and support their IT systems. Our clients include a full range of small, mid-size and larger businesses and many of our customer relationships span more than 2 decades. We have extensive experience working with both commercial and government entities such as MedStar Health, Regal Cinemas, Regus, USDA and many more.
